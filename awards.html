Awards & Honors

Rohit Sharma has been widely recognized for his contributions to Indian cricket. His consistent performances, leadership qualities, and sportsmanship have earned him numerous accolades.

National Honors

  • Arjuna Award (2015) – For his excellence in international cricket.
  • Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award (2020) – India’s highest sporting honor.

ICC Awards & Global Recognition

  • ICC ODI Player of the Year (2019).
  • Selected in ICC ODI Team of the Year multiple times (2014, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019).
  • ICC Men’s Cricketer of the Month (Feb 2021).

Franchise and IPL Awards

  • 5-time IPL Champion as captain of Mumbai Indians (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020).
  • Multiple Man of the Match and Man of the Tournament awards.

Special Mentions

  • First cricketer to score 3 double centuries in ODIs.
  • Most centuries (5) in a single World Cup edition (2019).
  • Honored with stadium stands and fan clubs in his name across India.
