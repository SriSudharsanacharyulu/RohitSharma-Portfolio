Records & Achievements

Rohit Sharma’s cricketing journey is studded with phenomenal records and milestones that have set him apart as one of the modern greats.

Batting Records

  • Most double centuries in ODIs: 3 (264, 209, 208*).
  • Highest individual ODI score: 264 vs Sri Lanka in 2014.
  • Fastest ODI century by an Indian: 35 balls vs Sri Lanka in 2017.
  • Most centuries in a single World Cup: 5 centuries in 2019.
  • Only Indian with 3 T20I centuries.
  • Second fastest T20I century globally: 35 balls vs Sri Lanka in 2017.

Captaincy Milestones

  • Led Mumbai Indians to 5 IPL titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020) — most by any captain.
  • Guided India to the Asia Cup 2018 title as stand-in captain.
  • Full-time captain of Indian ODI and T20 teams since 2022, known for calm and strategic leadership.

World Cup Achievements

  • Highest run scorer in the 2019 World Cup with 648 runs.
  • Key contributor to India's victory in the 2021 T20 World Cup semi-final and final.
  • Captain during 2024 T20 World Cup win, with crucial performances.
  • Part of the winning squad in the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy.

Unique Records

  • Only cricketer to score three double centuries in ODI cricket.
  • First batsman to score centuries in all three formats of international cricket multiple times.
  • Most IPL runs for Mumbai Indians — crossing 6000 runs.

Other Achievements

  • Recipient of the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award in 2020.
  • Awarded the Arjuna Award in 2015.
  • Multiple times recognized as ICC Player of the Month and Player of the Year in various categories.
